Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Lost Vac Bot ("we", "our", or "the App") handles information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Lost Vac Bot is designed with your privacy in mind.

Local Data Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • High scores and game progress
  • Game settings and preferences

This data is stored on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party. If you uninstall the App, this data will be deleted.

Third-Party Services

Lost Vac Bot does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services. We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

Children's Privacy

Lost Vac Bot does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is safe for users of all ages.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any personal data, there is no risk of your personal information being compromised through our App. All game data remains on your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" below.
